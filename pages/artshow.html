Here's how it works
1. You select a ready custom made frame from the shop, available April 25 - May 13, 2014
2. Complete entry form at the shop and select size: 11x14=$45, 16x20=$65, and 22x28=$85
3. There are four categories: Oil, Acrylic, Water color, and Mixed Media
4. Create your artwork
5. Bring you finished artwork and frame/mat back to the shop by May 14, 2014
6. Put a price on your artwork or mark it not for sale.
7. We will complete the framing of your artwork at no additional charge and hang your artwork in the show
8. The show is Sunday, May 18, 2014, 2-5 pm.


The rules
1. The shop will provide a selection of custom made frames and mats for participants
2. Registration and frame selections can be made any time after April 25, 2014
3. All show entries must be returned to the shop by 7:00 pm Wed May 14, 2014.
4. The shop will take a 10% commission on sales, if artwork sells at the show.
5. The shop hangs the show and provides a show booklet.
6. The shop will offer a $200 certificate to first place for each of the four categories, redeemable for future custom framing at Some Saturday Frame shop.
7. If your artwork does not sell, you may pick up your artwork during shop hours week of June 16-21, 2014.
